How do I subscribe to an RSS feed?

An RSS feed is an old internet kind of thing. I'll post new newsletters to the feed and anyone who is listening will get a notification. I can recommend the app 'Feedly', which allows you to put in the RSS link and will give you a little notification if I publish something.

Another alternative is I let you guys give me your email address and I learn how to send emails from here. I'm not excited about that because I don't want to collect any data (what if an oligarch blackmails me into selling your personal data?) and also because it's a bunch of work. If you really want that, let me know.

What's going into the newsletter?

I will aggregate all the new content, so I'm talking films, games, books etc, from the last time I sent a newsletter and put it in the post. I'll also give a short life update for your enjoyment as well. Maybe I'll have some other thoughts about life to share.
